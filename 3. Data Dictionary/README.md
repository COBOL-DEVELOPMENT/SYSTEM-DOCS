1. Applicant Information - The insurance applicant information for applying the policy.

2. Reject Customer D/B: The Underwriting department rejected to issue the policy, the database stores refused customer information.

3. Reject Customer Information: The applicant information who were rejected to issue the policy.

4. Customer D/B: This database consisted of all entire applicants. 

5. Underwriting Information: When the underwriting department inspected the application, it defined the decision of underwriting.  

6. Customer Information: The data of customer database used for processing application.

7. Premium Information: The actuarial department calculated the premium for the application.

8. Insurance Policy: After batch cycle, the applicant received the insurance policy.
